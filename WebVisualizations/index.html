Summary: Latitude vs. X



This project's data and visuals represent comparisions of what happens in four weather scenrios (Latitude vs. Max Temp, vs. Humidity, vs. Cloudiness and vs. Wind Speed) when you move closer to/farther from the equator. The data is from the OpenWeather API.
